## Emotional Experience Analysis

### 1. First Impression (Dashboard View)

**✅ EXCITEMENT LEVEL: HIGH**

**What Creates Excitement:**
- "🎉 New Offers (1)" - Celebration emoji immediately signals good news
- "⭐ Best Offer" badge - Makes creator feel valued
- Blue ring highlight (`ring-2 ring-primary-400`) - Visual emphasis draws attention
- Budget shown prominently in primary color (₹500) - Money is clear and visible

**What Reduces Anxiety:**
- Clear section header: "New Offers" - No confusion about what this is
- Brand name visible immediately: "Glow Skincare" - Real company, feels legitimate
- Status badge: "[New Offer]" - Clear state, no ambiguity
- Deadline shown: "Due Feb 15, 2024" - Time to decide, no pressure

**What Makes Next Step Obvious:**
- Entire card is clickable (hover effect)
- Arrow icon (→) on right side - Universal "go to" indicator
- Card is elevated and highlighted - Stands out from other content
- No competing CTAs in this section - Single focus

### 3. Accepting the Offer

**✅ SIMPLICITY LEVEL: HIGH**

**Action Required:**
1. Read the green card (all info repeated)
2. Click "Accept Offer" button
3. Done - status changes to "Content Creation"

**What Makes It Simple:**
- Single primary action: "Accept Offer"
- Secondary action clearly different: "Decline" (outline style)
- Button is full-width on mobile - Easy to tap
- Loading state shows immediately - No double-click confusion
- Success is immediate - Timeline updates, next step appears

**What Happens After Acceptance:**
